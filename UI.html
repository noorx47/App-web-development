About Nexen

Nexen was created with one simple idea — great technology should not cost a fortune. We believe that refurbished electronics, when properly tested and graded, offer the same experience as brand new devices at a fraction of the cost.

Every product on Nexen goes through a thorough inspection process before being listed. We grade each device based on its condition — Excellent, Good, or Fair — so you always know exactly what you are buying.
